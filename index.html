About Me

I am a nuclear astrophysicist specializing in high-resolution spectroscopy, chemodynamics, and the study of stellar populations in the Milky Way. My research focuses on the chemodynamical history of the Galactic halo, r-process nucleosynthesis, and the evolution of globular clusters. My expertise lies in uncovering the origins of chemical elements and tracing the progenitor supernovae that shape the chemical evolution of our galaxy.

Research Interests

  • High-resolution spectroscopy and kinematics of halo stars and globular clusters
  • r-process nucleosynthesis and progenitor supernovae tracing
  • Chemodynamical history and chemical evolution of the Milky Way
  • Data analysis of stellar populations to understand the Galactic structure
